TÍTULO: Structure-Guided Design of an Interface-Derived Inhibitor Peptide Against Spodoptera frugiperda Digestive Trypsins
AUTORES: Severiche-Castro J et al. (2026) Arch Insect Biochem Physiol 122:e70164
DOI: 10.1002/arch.70164
PMID: 42138598
PMC: PMC13178409
CATEGORIA: T1_alvo_peptideo
RELEVÂNCIA: ALTA — design in silico de peptídeo inibidor de tripsina de S. frugiperda; usa mesmo alvo do projeto

ABSTRACT:
Rational computational strategy identified PEP-11 (11-mer) targeting S. frugiperda digestive trypsin. MM/GBSA showed negative binding free energy in all replicas. Docking+MD revealed stable complex via polar and hydrophobic interactions.
